## Gap Categories

| Category | Definition |
|----------|------------|
| **BW** | Bodyweight-only options (equip level 0). Athlete has no equipment. |
| **DB** | Dumbbell-only options (equip level 1). Athlete has DBs/KBs/bands only. |
| **INT** | Intermediate progressions. Gap between difficulty levels is too large. |
| **ROT** | Rotational power options. Rotational=True or Rot secondary family. |
| **UNI** | Unilateral power options. Unilateral=True or unilateral variant. |
