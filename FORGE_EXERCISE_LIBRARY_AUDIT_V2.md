## Progression Quality Evaluation

**Pallof Press Progression Chain:**
1. Rot-001: Pallof Press (L1, cable/band, isometric anti-rotation)
2. Rot-002: Half-Kneeling Chop (L2, dynamic anti-rotation)
3. Rot-003: Standing Chop (L2, explosive anti-rotation)
4. → Missing: Standing Pallof Press (L3, standing variation)
5. → Missing: Pallof Press + Walkout (L4, eccentric anti-rotation)
6. → Missing: Single-Leg Pallof Press (L5, unilateral under anti-rotation)

**Assessment:** Good L1-L2 coverage. Gaps at L3-L5 where standing and unilateral
variations should live. The progression jumps from Pallof Press (L1) directly to
Half-Kneeling Chop (L2) — missing a standing Pallof Press as an intermediate step.

**Anti-Lateral Flexion Progression:**
1. Carry-001: Farmer Carry (L1, bilateral — no anti-lateral)
2. Carry-002: Suitcase Carry (L2, anti-lateral — offset load)
3. Carry-008: Cross-Body Carry (L3, anti-rotation + anti-lateral)
4. Carry-011: Suitcase Carry (offset) (L4, heavy anti-lateral)
5. → Missing: Single-Arm Overhead Carry (L4, requires full-body anti-lateral)
6. → Missing: Single-Arm Bottom-Up KB Carry (L5, extreme anti-lateral)

**Assessment:** Anti-lateral progression is solid. Missing the overhead single-arm carry as an L4 bridge.
