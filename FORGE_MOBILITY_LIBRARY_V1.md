# FORGE Mobility Library V1

**Purpose:** Structured mobility, activation, and stretching database for the
preparation and recovery layer. Mobility, activation, and stretching are
kept in separate sections — they are not interchangeable.

---

## Part 1 — Architecture

Three distinct categories. Do NOT mix them.

| Category | Purpose | When | Duration | Intensity |
|----------|---------|------|----------|-----------|
| **Mobility (M)** | Take a joint through its available range of motion | Warmup, pre-session | 5-10 min | Low, controlled |
| **Activation (A)** | Prime a muscle to fire before loading | Warmup, pre-lift | 3-5 min | Low-moderate |
| **Stretching (S)** | Lengthen a muscle after training | Post-session, recovery | 5-10 min | Low, held 30-60s |

### Key rules

1. **Mobility is not stretching.** Mobility moves a joint. Stretching
   lengthens a muscle. Different mechanisms, different timing.
2. **Activation precedes loading.** Activate a muscle before asking it to
   produce force. Never load a "cold" prime mover.
3. **Stretching is post-session only.** No static stretching before
   training (reduces force output for 60+ min).
4. **One mobility drill per major joint per session is sufficient.**
   Don't need 5 hip drills — pick the best one for today.
