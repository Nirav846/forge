# FORGE Testing Library V1

**Purpose:** Structured assessment system for speed, power, strength,
conditioning, and sport-specific testing. Designed for 2-4 tests per year
(not weekly). Includes equipment, reliability, norms, and frequency.

---

## Part 1 — Testing Principles

### 1.1 Frequency guidelines

| Test type | Frequency | When | Why so infrequent |
|-----------|-----------|------|-------------------|
| Speed | 2-4x per year | Start/end of each training block | Speed adapts slowly; weekly testing is noise, not signal |
| Power | 4x per year | Every 6-8 weeks | CMJ can be weekly (readiness), but maximal power tests need time between |
| Strength | 2-3x per year | Pre/ post block | Max strength testing is fatiguing; once per block is sufficient |
| Conditioning | 2-4x per year | Pre/ post block | Field tests are effort-dependent and exhausting |
| Sport-specific | 2-3x per year | Pre-season, mid-season, off-season | Depends on sport calendar |

### 1.2 Test selection rules

1. **Test what you train.** If you don't do broad jumps, don't test them.
2. **Fewer tests, better data.** One reliable test per quality is better
   than three unreliable tests.
3. **Standardise the protocol.** Same time of day, same warmup, same surface,
   same measurement method.
4. **Test fresh or don't test.** No testing after hard sessions or
   competition days.
5. **Normative data is directional, not diagnostic.** Use norms to spot
   outliers, not to label athletes.

## Part 2 — Speed Tests (5)

| ID | Test | Equipment | Reliability (CV) | Use case | Frequency | Normative references (male, elite) |
|----|------|-----------|-----------------|----------|-----------|-----------------------------------|
| SP-01 | 10m sprint | Timing gates, flat surface, spikes optional | 2-3% | Acceleration, first-step quickness | 2-4x/yr | < 1.70s (elite field sport), 1.70-1.85s (good), > 2.0s (needs work) |
| SP-02 | 20m sprint | Timing gates | 2-3% | Acceleration + early top speed | 2-4x/yr | < 2.90s (elite), 2.90-3.10s (good), > 3.30s (needs work) |
| SP-03 | 30m sprint | Timing gates | 2-3% | Max velocity | 2-4x/yr | < 4.00s (elite), 4.00-4.30s (good), > 4.50s (needs work) |
| SP-04 | Flying 10m (from 20m run-in) | Timing gates at 20m and 30m | 2-4% | Max velocity (true top speed) | 2-4x/yr | < 1.00s (elite), 1.00-1.10s (good), > 1.20s (needs work) |
| SP-05 | 5-10-5 pro-agility | Timing gates, cones | 3-5% | Change of direction speed | 3-4x/yr | < 4.30s (elite), 4.30-4.60s (good), > 5.00s (needs work) |

### Speed test protocols

**SP-01 to SP-03 (linear sprint):** 2-3 trials with full recovery (3+ min
between). Best trial recorded. Standing start, hands off ground. Same surface
each test.

**SP-04 (flying 10):** Athlete accelerates for 20m, timed from 20-30m mark.
Best of 2-3. Only valid if athlete is clearly still accelerating through the
timing zone.

**SP-05 (5-10-5):** 3-cone setup, 5 yards each direction. Best of 2 trials
each direction. Record direction-specific (left vs right).

## Part 3 — Power Tests (6)

| ID | Test | Equipment | Reliability (CV) | Use case | Frequency | Normative references |
|----|------|-----------|-----------------|----------|-----------|---------------------|
| PW-01 | CMJ (countermovement jump) | Force plate OR jump mat | 2-4% | Lower body power, movement quality, fatigue monitoring | Weekly (readiness) or 4x/yr (max) | 40-50cm (good team sport), 50-60cm (elite), > 65cm (exceptional) |
| PW-02 | CMJ (arm swing) | Force plate OR jump mat | 3-5% | Sport-specific jump height | 4x/yr | +5-10cm vs no-arm CMJ |
| PW-03 | Broad jump (standing) | Tape measure, firm surface | 3-5% | Horizontal power, asymmetry | 4x/yr | 2.30-2.50m (good), 2.50-2.70m (elite), > 2.80m (exceptional) |
| PW-04 | RSI (reactive strength index) | Force plate | 4-6% | Reactive strength, stiffness | 4x/yr | > 2.5 (excellent), 2.0-2.5 (good), < 1.5 (needs work) — 30cm drop |
| PW-05 | Med ball chest throw (seated) | 3-5kg med ball, tape | 4-6% | Upper body power | 4x/yr | 4-5m (3kg, good), 5-6m (elite) |
| PW-06 | Med ball rotational throw | 3-5kg med ball, wall | 5-7% | Rotational power | 4x/yr | Measure distance from wall; sport-specific norms |

### Power test protocols

**PW-01 (CMJ):** Hands on hips, jump for max height, land on same spot.
2-3 trials. Best height or average of best 2.

**PW-03 (broad jump):** Stand with toes behind line, jump as far forward
as possible, land on both feet. Measure from start line to back of rearmost
heel. 2-3 trials.

**PW-04 (RSI):** Drop from 30cm (or individualised height), minimise ground
contact time, maximise jump height. RSI = jump height (m) / contact time (s).
3-5 drops.

## Part 4 — Strength Tests (5)

| ID | Test | Equipment | Reliability (CV) | Use case | Frequency | Normative references |
|----|------|-----------|-----------------|----------|-----------|---------------------|
| ST-01 | Trap bar deadlift (3-5RM) | Trap bar, plates | 3-5% | Full body strength, hip hinge | 2-3x/yr | 1.8-2.2x BW (good), 2.2-2.5x BW (elite) |
| ST-02 | Back squat (3-5RM) | Squat rack, barbell | 3-5% | Lower body strength | 2-3x/yr | 1.5-1.8x BW (good), 1.8-2.2x BW (elite) |
| ST-03 | Pull-up (max reps) | Pull-up bar | 5-8% | Upper body pulling endurance | 2-3x/yr | 12-15 reps (good), 15-20 (elite), > 20 (exceptional) |
| ST-04 | Pull-up (max weight, 1RM) | Pull-up bar, weight belt | 4-6% | Upper body pulling strength | 2-3x/yr | 1.2-1.4x BW (good), 1.4-1.6x BW (elite) |
| ST-05 | Isometric mid-thigh pull (IMTP) | Force plate + rack | 3-5% | Full body isometric strength, force production | 2-4x/yr | > 3000N (good), > 4000N (elite, depends on BW) |

### Strength test protocols

**ST-01 / ST-02 (RM testing):** Ramp up over 4-5 sets (50%, 70%, 85%, 90%,
95% estimated 1RM), then attempt 3-5RM. Use a calculator to estimate 1RM
(Epley formula: weight × (1 + reps/30)). Use standardised depth (THigh
parallel, box to 90° for squat).

**ST-03 (pull-up max reps):** Pronated grip, dead hang start, chin over bar
each rep, no kipping. Count clean reps.

**ST-04 (weighted pull-up):** Add weight in 2.5-5kg increments, 3-5 min rest
between attempts.

**ST-05 (IMTP):** Pull against immovable bar at mid-thigh height. 3-5s max
effort. 2-3 trials. Peak force recorded.

## Part 5 — Conditioning Tests (5)

| ID | Test | Equipment | Reliability (CV) | Use case | Frequency | Normative references |
|----|------|-----------|-----------------|----------|-----------|---------------------|
| CD-01 | MAS (30-15 IFT) | Cones, 40m course, beep audio | 3-4% | Maximal aerobic speed, field-based | 2-4x/yr | 19-21 km/h (good), 21-23 (elite field sport), < 18 (needs work) |
| CD-02 | Yo-Yo IR1 | Cones, audio | 4-5% | Repeated high-intensity endurance | 2-3x/yr | Level 20-22 (good), 22-24 (elite), > 24 (exceptional) |
| CD-03 | Bronco | Cones, 20m course | 3-4% | Repeated effort endurance (rugby-specific) | 2-3x/yr | < 5:00 (elite rugby), 5:00-5:30 (good) |
| CD-04 | Beep test (MSFT) | Cones, 20m course, audio | 4-5% | General aerobic capacity | 2-3x/yr | Level 13-14 (good), 14-15 (elite) |
| CD-05 | Cooper test (12 min run) | 400m track or GPS | 4-6% | Aerobic capacity, large groups | 2-3x/yr | 2700-3000m (good), 3000-3400m (elite), > 3400m (exceptional) |

### Conditioning test protocols

**CD-01 (MAS):** 30-15 intermittent fitness test. 40m shuttles with 15s
recovery. Increases speed by 0.5 km/h every stage. Last completed stage =
MAS (maximal aerobic speed). Used to set training paces.

**CD-02 (Yo-Yo IR1):** 2 × 20m shuttles with increasing speed. 10s active
recovery between shuttles. Level + shuttle = final score.

**CD-03 (Bronco):** 3 shuttles (20m + 40m + 60m) = 1 rep. 5 reps with 30s
recovery between. Total time recorded.

## Part 7 — Testing Battery Templates

### 7.1 Off-season intake (comprehensive, 2 sessions)

| Session | Tests | Time |
|---------|-------|------|
| Day 1 (fresh) | SP-01, SP-02, SP-04, PW-01, PW-03, ST-05 (IMTP) | 75 min |
| Day 2 (fresh) | ST-01 or ST-02, CD-01 or CD-02, sport-specific | 90 min |

### 7.2 In-season monitoring (minimal, 30 min)

| Session | Tests | Time | When |
|---------|-------|------|------|
| Weekly | PW-01 (CMJ) only | 5 min | Pre-session, Monday |
| Monthly | SP-01 (10m) + PW-03 (broad jump) | 15 min | Fresh day |

### 7.3 Pre-competition (minimal, 20 min)

| Session | Tests | Time |
|---------|-------|------|
| Single session | SP-01, PW-01, CD-02 (Yo-Yo level only, not to exhaustion) | 20 min |

## Part 8 — Summary

|----------|-------|-----------------|
| Speed | 5 | SP-01 to SP-05, timing gates required |
| Power | 6 | PW-01 to PW-06, force plate optional for most |
| Strength | 5 | ST-01 to ST-05, RM or isometric |
| Conditioning | 5 | CD-01 to CD-05, field-based, audio required for 3 |
| Cricket | 4 | SS-CR-01 to SS-CR-04 |
| Tennis | 3 | SS-TN-01 to SS-TN-03 |

**Total tests: 31**

Each test includes equipment, reliability (CV %), use case, frequency, and
normative references. Testing is 2-4x per year for most qualities. Weekly
monitoring is limited to CMJ (readiness) only.
