# FORGE Recovery Library V1

**Purpose:** Structured recovery protocols classified L1-L5 by fatigue level.
Covers circuits, regeneration, pool, bike, mobility, travel, tournament,
and post-match recovery.

---

## Part 1 — Architecture

Recovery is prescribed based on athlete-reported or coach-observed fatigue
level, not on a fixed schedule.

### L1-L5 classification

| Level | Fatigue | When | Recovery type | Duration | Coach action |
|-------|---------|------|---------------|----------|-------------|
| L1 | Low (normal training) | After standard session | Minimal — mobility + stretch | 10 min | No change to next session |
| L2 | Moderate (hard session) | After heavy lifting, speed | Active recovery — bike/pool/walk | 20 min | Reduce volume next session by 10% |
| L3 | High (competition, travel) | After match, tournament day | Structured recovery circuit | 30 min | Reduce volume next session by 20% |
| L4 | Very high (multi-day tournament) | Tournament block | Full regeneration protocol | 45 min | Light session or off next day |
| L5 | Extreme (fatigue, travel + comp) | End of tour, heavy block | Rest day + recovery modalities | Off + 30 min | Mandatory rest day |

### Recovery type by fatigue level

| Type | L1 | L2 | L3 | L4 | L5 |
|------|----|----|----|----|----|
| Mobility + stretch | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Bike recovery | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | — |

## Part 3 — Pool Recovery (4 protocols)

| ID | Protocol | Level | Duration | When | Details |
|----|----------|-------|----------|------|---------|
| PR-01 | Pool walk/jog | L2-L3 | 15-20 min | After training | Walk in chest-deep water, progressive to high-knee jog. 2 min walk, 1 min jog x 5-7 |
| PR-02 | Deep water running | L3-L4 | 20 min | Heavy session, travel | Flotation belt, aqua jog. 5 min easy, 10 min steady, 5 min easy |
| PR-03 | Pool recovery circuit | L3-L4 | 25 min | Tournament, post-match | 5 exercises x 45s each: pool walk, high knees, side shuffle, cross-over, backwards jog |
| PR-04 | Cold water immersion | L4-L5 | 10-12 min | Post-competition, extreme fatigue | 10-15°C, 2 min in + 1 min out x 3-4 circuits. Do not do after strength session (blunts adaptation) |

## Part 6 — Travel Recovery (4 protocols)

### 6.1 Air travel recovery (L3-L4)

| Phase | Action | Duration | Notes |
|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| In-flight | Compression socks, hydration, walk every 60 min | Flight | Avoid alcohol |
| On arrival | 15 min walk + hydration + light mobility | 20 min | MR-04 preferred |
| Evening | BR-01 (easy spin) + full recovery circuit | 35 min | RC-04 |
| Next AM | Light activation + mobility | 15 min | Hip CARs, ankle CARs, cat-cow |

### 6.2 Bus/car travel recovery (L2-L3)

| Phase | Action | Duration | Notes |
|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| En route | Get out and walk at rest stops | Every 2 hr | 5 min walk |
| On arrival | MR-01 (general mobility) | 10 min | Focus on hips and low back |
| Evening | RC-01 (general recovery circuit) | 20 min | |

### 6.3 Tournament travel recovery (L4)

| ID | Protocol | Duration | When | Details |
|----|----------|----------|------|---------|
| TR-01 | Travel day regeneration | 40 min | Evening of travel day | BR-01 (15 min) + PR-01 (15 min) + MR-04 (10 min) |
| TR-02 | Multi-day tournament recovery | 30 min | Each evening of tournament | PR-02 (15 min) + RC-04 (15 min). Sleep extension 9+ hrs |

### 6.4 Post-match recovery (L3-L4)

| Phase | Timing | Action | Duration |
|-------|--------|--------|----------|
| Immediate | 0-15 min post | Hydration + carb/protein refuel + light walk | 15 min |
| Early | 15-60 min post | Compression + elevation if needed + light mobility | 20 min |
| Later | 2-4 hr post | PR-01 (pool) or BR-01 (bike) + full recovery circuit | 40 min |
| Evening | After meal | RC-02 (lower body recovery) + sleep prep | 25 min |
